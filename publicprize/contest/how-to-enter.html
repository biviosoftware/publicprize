How To Enter

  1. Register. Fill out a short entry form.

  2. Deliver. Upload your pitch video to YouTube and slide deck to slideshare. Add the sharing links to your entry form.

  3. Communicate. Answer questions from the community.

Deadline: You must submit your complete entry by Midnight, October 16th, 2014.

Finalists will be listed here on October 21st, 2014.

Winners will be announced here and at the Esprit Entrepreneur Ceremony on October 23rd, 2014 from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. at the NEED LOCATION. Finalists must attend the ceremony so please keep this date open.

Rules

Seed Stage
The Esprit Venture Prize is designed for early stage startups, which have received at most $1,500,000 in seed funding and $2,000,000 in total revenue. Entrants must be fully formed corporations or partnerships with a federal tax identification number.
Boulder Ventures Only
The Esprit Venture Prize is a Boulder Chamber of Commerce initiative to promote innovation in Boulder, Colorado, US. Entrants must have at least one Boulder County resident who is a founder, an active contributor to the business, and owns at least 20% of the business.
Public Contest
The Esprit Venture Prize is a Public Prize contest All the contest materials will be published for public review on the Public Prize website (publicprize.com). Certain information such as federal tax identifiers and email addresses will only be visible to Public Prize and Boulder Chamber administrators. Passwords will in all cases be stored in the database in an encrypted form.
Taxes
All Prizes are inclusive of any tax, levy or other charge that may arise under any applicable law. The Winner acknowledges that they will not be entitled to demand any additional payment by reason of a Prize being subject to any tax, levy or other charge in any jurisdiction.
