The Esprit Venture Prize is a crowdfunded contest for seed-stage companies headquartered in Boulder, CO. There are three prizes given away: first, second, and third. The winners are determined by the votes of the donors. Anybody can donate.

Startups submit entries including a pitch video, a pitch deck, a summary, and founder profiles. Donors may ask anything they like of the starutps. The founders answers will be found on the startup's contestant page.
