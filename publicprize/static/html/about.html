Boulder's Own Startup Competition

The Boulder Chamber’s Esprit Venture Challenge is a community-oriented startup competition with a twist. Everyone gets a say in who wins. You help drive Boulder’s innovation by helping select the most promising startup for a top prize of $9,000.

Boulder is renowned as a community that works to address global issues through innovative solutions. Joining in that spirit, the Venture Challenge will be an exclusive competition among aspiring social entrepreneurs. Each competing company will be judged based on its promise as a social entrepreneur as well as on its potential for sustainable ROI.

It’s all about the engaged “Boulderpreneur!”

And new this year, the Boulder Chamber is encouraging our young social entrepreneurs with a special student competition. Open only to student-led businesses, it allows them to compete for a separate prize of $1000. And they’d still be eligible to compete for more.

Why Get Involved?

An outstanding success in its inaugural competition, Esprit Venture Challenge aims to help spotlight and reward Boulder’s most promising and innovative new businesses. This year, the Challenge is furthering Boulder’s cherished values of social benefit—making the world a better place by encouraging “Social Entrepreneurism”—doing well by doing good! We’ll demonstrate that America’s startup capital is also America’s leader in making a positive impact through business success.

We’ll put our world-renowned collection of creative scientists, engineers, business leaders, artists, athletes and nonprofits to work helping solve world problems, big and small. All the contestants will use their business acumen to help improve everyday lives while growing tomorrow’s economic powerhouses . . .all the while, contributing to Boulder’s economic vitality.

How Does It Work?

The Esprit Venture Challenge is for seed-stage businesses: startups with less than two years in operation. This year, they must have social entrepreneurship as their principal business purpose, along with a clear model for achieving a sustainable return on investment.
