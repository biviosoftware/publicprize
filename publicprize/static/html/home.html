About Esprit Entrepreneur

For the 31st year the Boulder Chamber is proud to present Esprit, the premier celebration of the Entrepreneur in Boulder. This year’s event is an exciting revisioning of the celebration and includes collaboration with NewCo, an up close experience of Boulder’s leading companies and start ups. The Esprit Celebration will be held on November 18th from 5-8:30PM in the University of Colorado’s newly opened Touchdown Club overlooking the north end zone of Folsom Field. Esprit will feature a networking cocktail reception from 5-6PM followed by a Jeffersonian Dinner during which our guests from all areas of the Boulder Economy will engage in a discussion of the issues of the day.
