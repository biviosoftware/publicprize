About Esprit Entrepreneur

For the 32nd year the Boulder Chamber is proud to present Esprit, the premier celebration of the Entrepreneur in Boulder. This year’s event is an exciting revisioning of the celebration and includes collaboration with NewCo, an up close experience of Boulder’s leading companies and start ups. The Esprit Celebration will be held on October 25th from 5-8:30 p.m. in the University of Colorado’s Memorial Center. Esprit will feature a networking cocktail reception from 5-6PM followed by a Jeffersonian Dinner during which our guests from all areas of the Boulder Economy will engage in a discussion of the issues of the day.
